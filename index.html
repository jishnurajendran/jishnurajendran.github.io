About

My name is Jishnu Rajendran. I am a PhD Student at Department of Physics and Astronomy “Ettore Majorana”, Università degli Studi di Catania. Prior to this, I completed my Integrated M.Sc program from National Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhubansewar. My M.Sc thesis was on Quantum Computation particularly Quantum walks and Quantum games under the supervision of Dr. Colin Benjamin.
